Founder photos
==============

Drop each founder's portrait in this folder, then point to it from
src/data/team.ts via the `image` field, e.g. "/team/founder-1.jpg".

Guidelines for a clean, consistent grid:
- Square crop, roughly 600 x 600 px (head & shoulders, centered).
- Same lighting / background style across all four for a cohesive row.
- .jpg or .webp, optimized to keep the page fast (< ~150 KB each).

Suggested filenames (match the order in team.ts):
  founder-1.jpg
  founder-2.jpg
  founder-3.jpg
  founder-4.jpg

Until a photo is added, the card automatically shows the founder's
initials as a placeholder — no broken images.
